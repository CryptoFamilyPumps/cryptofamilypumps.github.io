How Does Our Group Work?

CryptoFamily is a collection of members that seek to pump cryptocurrencies in a controlled manner, and in the process of doing so can boost the coins by more than 200%. At face value, it sounds as if it would be easy to do, but we do warn that it is always best to read and learn more about the approaches to help increase the profit you can make in our discord server.

The pump team monitors the market and then ultimately decides on the coin that we will pump. It will be chosen according to the market conditions, volume projection, and other factors. This is then followed by us launching a countdown to the pump itself. As the countdown hits zero, members of the group make a mass purchase of the coin to then inflate the price.

At the same time, the members are seeking to encourage outsiders to join them in buying the coins as the price rises with the opportunity for us to effectively dump the coins to the outsiders and create a profit within the group. We are by community consensus of all pump groups the best at bringing in outsiders. Our members are dedicated, our news is believable, our links are all working, and we incorporate the use of specialty built tools to help spread FOMO around the crypto world.

On average, a pump is only going to last somewhere in the region of 5-10 minutes. However, even if you set your sell too high and do not reach it durring this time, our track record shows that the coins we focus on for the pump have a tendency to then increase in price again after several days allowing you to still make your profit. Never ever sell at a loss.

We generally commit to 2-3 pumps per week.
