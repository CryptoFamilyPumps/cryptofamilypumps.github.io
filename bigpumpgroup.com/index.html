How Does Our Group Work?

Big Pump Group is a collection of members that seek to pump cryptocurrencies in a controlled manner, and in the process of doing so can boost the coins by more than 100%. At face value, it sounds as if it would be easy to do, but we do warn that it is always best to read and learn more about the approaches to help increase the profit you can make.

The pump team monitors the market and then ultimately decides on the coin that we will pump. It will be chosen according to the graphs and also news surrounding the coin. This is then followed by us launching a countdown to the pump itself. As the countdown hits zero, members of the group make a mass purchase of the coin in question to then inflate the price.

At the same time, the members are seeking to encourage outsiders to join them in buying the coins as the price rises with the opportunity for us to effectively dump the coins to the outsiders and create a profit within the group.

On average, a pump is only going to last somewhere in the region of 5 minutes. However, even if you do not sell again within that time, our track record shows that the coins we focus on for the pump have a tendency to then increase in price again after several days allowing you to still make your profit.

Also, we generally commit to two or three different pumps and coins in a single week.
