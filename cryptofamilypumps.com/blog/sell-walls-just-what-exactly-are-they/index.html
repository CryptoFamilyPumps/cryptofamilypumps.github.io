Sell Walls – Just What Exactly are They?

If you are planning on becoming involved in cryptocurrency trading on a regular basis, then being aware of something known as sell walls is going to prove to be rather important. After all, knowledge is indeed power, and when that power is directly linked to your ability to make money, then it becomes even more important than ever before.

The quick answer, is that a sell wall is a tool by which the sell price of a cryptocurrency can be manipulated so that it decreases in value very quickly. However, a large sell wall has to be created, with it placed at a set price, in order to stop higher sell orders from actually being executed. As a result, the person creating the sell wall is going to have greater control over what happens.

So, that all sounds like a lot of complex work, so how does it actually work?

The easiest way to discuss this is to provide you with an example. Let’s say a wealthy cryptoinvestor with money to burn tells a couple of his friends that he plans on making money on a cryptocurrency and wants them to be involved. He perhaps sees that cryptocurrency that he has his eyes on as having real potential for the future along with the simple fact that it could be rather easy to manipulate it.

At this point, there are three of them involved, and they plan on purchasing around 1 million tokens of the desired currency, but there’s an issue. If they go out and purchase all of those tokens, then it makes sense that the currency is going to go up in price rather quickly, and that is going against their aims.

To counteract this, there is a clear plan to put into place that will keep things more firmly under control.

First, they make a series of small purchases and they split it up between the three of them with it totalling perhaps 200k tokens for each individual. This will mean that the price is not going to skyrocket like it otherwise would do. Then, they look at setting the sell wall, which is the price at which they plan on selling their tokens as they know that the price will drop and they can then purchase their 1 million tokens.

So, they get the price, and with there being 600k tokens being sold, it does mean that this is going to be big enough for it to have an impact on the overall market rate. They sell at the rate, and the price drops as other sellers see that it is going down and know that they cannot set the price above that sell wall rate.

By doing this, it pushes the price down even further creating the opportunity for our team of investors to then purchase their 1 million tokens at a low price. Once the sell wall is effectively brought down, the price rises once again and they then have the tokens that they want without paying over the odds for them in any way. This will then, in turn, make it easier for them to produce a huge profit or hold onto them for longer and create an even larger and more impressive investment.

But How Does it Help?

To understand how it all works, and how it then helps our investors, we need to think about a common human trait, which is called fear. People see that the price of something is going down, so they panic and feel that they will lose everything. This then leads to them taking actions that they had perhaps not wanted to take as they feel that they are forced into selling.

By selling, the price goes even lower, which is good news for our investors. The lower it goes, the bigger the potential profit that can be made when you snap up that large quantity of tokens.

So, How Do You Take Advantage?

Here’s an important question, how will it be possible for you to take advantage of this entire thing yourself? Well, drop the idea that you need to be some huge investor as that’s not the case. Instead, some careful planning on your part is going to play a key role.

First, you need to find a clear sell wall and this is often a cryptocurrency that is getting good press but the price is just not acting in the way that it should and is stagnant. Spend some time studying the depth charts that appear on various exchanges to help you identify if there is a sell wall in place.

When you find one, then you should invest what you can afford, and also what you can afford to lose should it go wrong, and then it is all about paying close attention to the market. This part is just showing patience and playing the waiting game as you have to hold on until the large investors have finished doing their thing since you know you are going to still make your own profit out of all of this.

The key is to wait until all of that pressure to sell has been released as that is when the price really does start to bounce back up quite rapidly. At this point, you can then sell and make your profit meaning you have been able to really take advantage of the actions of some of the largest players on the market.

What we are saying is that a sell wall is not something that you should fear simply because there are ways and means to make a profit even without you purchasing a vast number of tokens of a particular currency. The hardest part is in identifying those sell walls, but perfect that part and you will certainly be onto a good thing.

This is easier to do than you think, and the potential is huge if you are able to get in at the right time and have the patience to hold on until it is time to sell. If this is possible, then look forward to a substantial profit.
